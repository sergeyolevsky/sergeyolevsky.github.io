Corporate Culture: Bureaucrats, Stop-Managers and Fintech
November 2025

In most companies, the way the business operates and the way people work inside it is a tangled mix of objective and subjective factors. Hidden shareholder conflicts, decorative maneuvers by top management, the fears and insecurities of mid-level leaders, and internal micro-cultures that nobody acknowledges but everyone feels.

But the nuances begin immediately: some people are willing to take risks, others fear them; some see opportunities, others see only threats. In fintech this tension is even sharper: the market moves faster than internal approvals, and friction becomes inevitable.

Not necessarily Branson or Jobs ‚Äî that is a high bar. But at least in strategic vision and public leadership. A company ultimately reflects the personality of its leader.
