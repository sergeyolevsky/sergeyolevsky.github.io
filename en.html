Sergei Olevskii

Practitioner in Global Payments, Emerging Markets, and Strategic Development

Sergei Olevskii is a practitioner in international payments and cross-border finance with over 15 years of experience. He has worked with Transfast (later acquired by Mastercard) and advised Earthport, focusing on payment infrastructure and compliance in emerging markets.

Educated in the United States and Canada, and a participant in programs at the Oxford Saïd Business School, he has been involved in creating and developing projects across the CIS, the Middle East, and Africa — including the development of a columbite (Nb-Ta) mining project in Nigeria.

Today, Sergei works at the intersection of finance, technology, and geo-economics, exploring new approaches to international settlements and global development strategies.
