Sergei Olevskii

Practitioner in Global Payments and Strategic Development

Born in Moscow, Sergei played hockey for the junior team of Spartak Moscow. A practitioner with over 15 years of experience in international payments and money transfers. He worked at Transfast (later acquired by Mastercard) and advised Earthport — a former IFC (World Bank Group) equity entity, now operating under the Visa Direct brand — focusing on payment infrastructure, compliance, and global settlements. He participated in an expert working group under the Bank of Russia that supported the preparation of the federal law “On the National Payment System” (161-FZ). Educated in the United States and Canada, he also attended programs at Oxford Saïd Business School. He has been involved in building and developing projects across the CIS, the Middle East, and Nigeria, where he worked on the development of a columbite (Nb-Ta) deposit and remains connected to this field. Today, at the intersection of technology, finance, and geo-economics, he explores new approaches to international settlements and global development strategies.
