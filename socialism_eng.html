Where socialism was actually implemented

The USSR, Eastern Europe, Cuba, Venezuela, and Cambodia under Pol Pot ‚Äî all followed different cultural and historical paths, but faced the same structural issue: without market incentives, innovation declines, productivity falls, and the economy slows down.

Sweden, Norway, and Denmark are not socialist states. They are market economies where:

  • entrepreneurship and competition are encouraged,

Key difference
